    CatnMouse

    APK and AppInventor files for CatnMouse, a dodge game

    CatnMouse is an Android game made using AppInventor. You are the mouse, and you avoid the cat who is chasing you for as long as you can. There are cheese power ups to help you stay away. The app remembers your previous times and there are skins to choose from!

    NOTE: CatnMouse is no longer in development. We felt we accomplished all we could with AppInventor and adding more features would best be done from scratch.

    Check out the screenshots and APK in the repository.
